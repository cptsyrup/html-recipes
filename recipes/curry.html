Curry Rice

Curry Rice is one of my fav japanese dishes, and it's relatively easy to make!

Ingredients

  • Curry Roux
  • Broth/Stock
  • Vegetables
  • Protein
  • Rice

Instructions

  1. Cook white rice
  2. Boil broth and add vegtables and protein
  3. Add roux and let simmer for 15-20 min
  4. Pour over rice and enjoy!
