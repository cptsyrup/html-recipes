Miso Soup

Homemade miso soup is excellent for the body and soul!

Ingredients

  • Hondashi
  • Silken Tofu
  • Seaweed
  • Red Miso Paste
  • Scallion

Instructions

  1. Simmer water and hondashi to create broth
  2. Add cubed tofu, and seaweed to broth
  3. Turn down heat and add miso paste and mix
  4. Garnish with sliced scallion
