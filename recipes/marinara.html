Marinara

Homemade marinara sauce is so delicious and very easy to make!

Ingredients

  • Canned San Marzano Tomatoes
  • Olive Oil
  • Fresh Basil
  • Salt
  • Fresh Garlic

Instructions

  1. Slice Garlic
  2. Crush tomatoes
  3. Heat olive oil in pan then add garlic
  4. Add crushed tomatoes and 1/2 cup of water
  5. Add basil on top and simmer for 15-20 min
  6. Mix in your fav pasta and enjoy!
